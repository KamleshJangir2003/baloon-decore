Our Story

We Started With One
Dream & One Balloon

Shri was founded in 2019 with a simple belief — every celebration deserves to look magical, no matter the budget. What started as a small team decorating birthday parties in Mumbai has grown into India's most trusted balloon decoration brand.

Today we operate across 50+ cities with 100+ trained decorators who bring creativity, precision & heart to every single setup. From intimate room decorations to grand stage setups — we handle it all.

Our promise: on-time setup, premium quality, and a decoration that makes your guests go "wow" the moment they walk in.

Our Journey

How Far We've Come

From a single city to all of India — here's our story in milestones.

🎈
2019

Shri is Born

Started with 2 decorators and 50 balloons in a small Mumbai flat. First event was a kids birthday party.

🚀
2020

500 Events Milestone

Despite the pandemic, we pivoted to intimate home setups and crossed 500 events. Customers loved the personal touch.

🌆
2021

Expanded to 10 Cities

Launched in Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad & Chennai. Hired 30+ decorators to meet demand.

⭐
2022

1000+ 5-Star Reviews

Crossed 1000 five-star reviews across Google and WhatsApp. Became the most reviewed balloon brand in India.

🇮🇳
2024

50+ Cities & 5000+ Events

Now serving 50+ cities with 100+ decorators and 5000+ events decorated. The journey continues!
